The Essence of Yoga

Yoga is a spiritual, mental, and physical practice that originated in ancient India. It involves disciplines such as breath control, meditation, and specific bodily postures to achieve spiritual insight and tranquility.

Types of Yoga

  • Hatha Yoga
  • Raja Yoga
  • Karma Yoga
  • Bhakti Yoga
  • Jnana Yoga

Benefits

Regular practice of Yoga leads to improved physical health, mental clarity, and spiritual growth.

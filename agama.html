Introduction to Tantra Agama

Tantra Agama refers to a collection of esoteric traditions and scriptures that focus on spiritual practices, rituals, and philosophy, often involving the worship of deities and the use of mantras, mudras, and yantras.

Key Aspects

  • Worship and Rituals
  • Mantra and Yantra
  • Spiritual Transformation

Philosophical Teachings

Tantra emphasizes the importance of direct experience and realization, often transcending conventional religious boundaries.
